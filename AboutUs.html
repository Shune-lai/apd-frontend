THE FUTURE OF FOOD IS RADICALLY REAL

We’re building a world where everything is fresh from the earth, where supply chains are sustainable, and people are connected to the food they eat. For real.

Real goodness in every bite

Erik Battes might be a Michelin star chef — but he also believes that the best dishes aren’t fancy or frilly. That’s why every RealEats recipe is simply prepared with whole ingredients for a back-to-basics approach and an anything-but-basic taste.

Healthy food for every household

A single dad who wanted more for his sons than frozen pizza for dinner every night, Dan Wise founded MerryMeals after stumbling upon sous vide, a decades-old culinary method that locks in nutrients and flavor — and infuses every Fresh Pack™ with a whole world of possibility.
